About

Our Spare Parts & Blue Prints aim to help make school, longer homework assignments, and homework, in general, be less stressful. The advice and resource section is a place for students to share some advice, tips, or resources that they wish they would have known when they ran into some homework/school trouble. If a resource or a piece of advice is popular or helpful it could potentially become a Study Bot or apart of a Study Bot. If you have some advice, tips, or a resource that you want to be posted on our Resource & Advice page, we'd be more then happy to help share it! Email us at studybots1@gmail.com or fill out the form below. Your real name will be kept anonymous unless you'd like us to use a username you make up.
