About Study Bots

The After School Helpers.

Study Bots aims to make homework and studying easier and enjoyable for students in 1st-5th grade. By equipping students with basic organization and study skills needed for school when there young it will set them up for healthier and effective study habits in the future.

Meet the Developer

sprz05

9th grade Developer of StudyBots

Hello! I am the developer of Study Bots! When I was young, homework and studying were very confusing tasks. I felt like I was alone. I had no one to help me cope with the stress that came with homework. I began to despise it. It took me to long to do, I didn't like doing it, and I certainly wasn't motivated to do it. I look back now and I realize that my studying and homework habits caused all of my problems. I've invented Study Bot to give students a point in the right direction when it comes to studying and homework.
