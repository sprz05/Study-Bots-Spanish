ITHB

Interval Timer Homework Bot

ITHB, aim to help younger students establish healthy and effective study habits. Most students dread studying because we’re taught at a young age that it means sitting at a desk for hours. When in reality it’s not. This method of studying is both unhealthy and ineffective. ITHB aims to establish healthy and effective study. By breaking up study/homework sessions into short sprints with breaks in between (e.g. 30 minutes of studying, 10 minute break, repeat 2 times) It will help students recharge their batteries and keep them on task. Which will allow them to learn better and finish homework faster.
