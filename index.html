Study Bots

Our bots are designed and built to make homework and study time more enjoyable, healthy and effective.

ITHB

Use ITHB while your doing homework.

ITHB (Interval Timer Homework Bot), Aims to make studying effective and fun.

Go to ITHB

HOB

Hob a simple robot here to help organize your homework!

Use HOB while your doing homework.

Go to HOB

SLAB

SLAB aims to help make school, longer homework assignments, and homework, in general, be less stressful. All of the spare parts and blue prints are from students!

Use SLAB when you need or want to share some advice/work for school.

Go to SLAB

WWC

Done with homework, don't have anything to do? World Wide Clubs can help! It hosts clubs on our website.You can join one, see what clubs are making, meet new students, or start your own club!

You can use WWC when your done with homework!

Go to WWC
