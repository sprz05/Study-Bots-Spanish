SLAB

SLAB aims to help make school, longer homework assignments, and homework, in general, be less stressful. It shares tricks/trips/resources from students!

Use SLAB when you need or want to share some advice/work for school.

Q+A:

What are routines?

Routines that use bots in study Bots. They aim to build healthy homework/studying routines for students.

What are bots?

Bots aim to help show students what healthy study habits look like. They also used in our routines.

What else do you offer?

We offer homework help. It gives students a place to ask questions they have with homework. Currently we only help with math homework, but as our communtiy grows we'll cover more topics!
